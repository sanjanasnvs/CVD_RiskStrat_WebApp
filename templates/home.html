About CVD Risk Predictor

Our cardiovascular disease risk prediction platform represents the cutting edge of medical technology machine learning. Developed through extensive research and training on the UK Biobank dataset, we aim to provide healthcare professionals and patients with accurate, reliable risk assessments.

By combining multiple machine learning models with evidence-based medical guidelines, we leverage self-reported data to deliver personalized insights that help individuals understand their cardiovascular health and take proactive steps toward prevention.

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced cardiovascular risk assessment tools while maintaining the highest standards of medical accuracy and data security.

  • Clinically validated prediction models
  • Real-time risk assessment and reporting
  • Integration with healthcare workflows
  • Continuous model improvement and updates

Whether you're a healthcare professional seeking advanced tools for risk stratification or an individual interested in understanding your cardiovascular health, our platform provides the insights you need to make informed decisions about heart health.
